Her Safety

In our collaborative effort on the 'HerSafety' project, we developed a comprehensive digital solution to enhance women's safety. The project included features such as SOS alerts, location tracking, and fake calls. This innovative solution focused on user-friendly approaches and global accessibility, addressing safety issues across diverse regions. We conducted surveys to identify gaps in existing solutions and implemented features to ensure comprehensive protection and usability.

Student Management System

Developed a Java-based "Student Activity Management System" to manage student results for three Semester 1 modules. This menu-driven system enables staff to check seats, register/delete students, store/load student data, and view sorted lists for up to 100 students using arrays and methods. Enhanced with classes and objects, it manages module marks and grades, and includes reporting features to generate summaries and performance reports sorted by average marks, showcasing skills in Java, OOP, and algorithms.

Good Health and Wellbeing

Our "Good Health and Wellbeing" project features a range of web pages, including a Splash Screen, Home Page, Gallery, Shop, User Profile, Feedback, Sitemap, Team page, Content Page, and Page Editor. Developed using JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, the project is designed to offer users a comprehensive experience on health and wellness topics. Additionally, the project includes a CV section detailing the team members' roles and contributions, highlighting their skills and involvement in creating this interactive and informative platform.
